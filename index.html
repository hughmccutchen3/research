Why it is different

Our methodology and platform make the researcher the leader of a capable team — analysts, writers, fact-checkers. The process is open, the platform and tools are open, and the results make clear how the work was done. The proof is in the work itself.

Every published piece carries the Bursera mark and a provenance record you can inspect — how the work was done, what it rests on, and who is accountable for it.

Human-led, disclosed

Every engagement is led by a named analyst — one accountable person directing the work, reviewing it critically, and deciding when it is ready. We tell you where the AI did the lifting and where human judgment made the call. The judgment is the point, and it is never anonymous.
