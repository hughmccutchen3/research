Research you can trust

Bursera Consulting takes a business question, builds the evidence base, and returns insights with the sources attached — clear about where the evidence is solid and where it is thin. The work runs on a research platform built around large language models, directed and checked by the analyst at every step.

What Bursera does

Research, analysis, and writing. You bring a question your organization needs answered well: a market to understand, a claim to pressure-test, a decision that needs an evidence base under it. Bursera returns work you can act on — a foundational evidence base you keep, applied deep-dives on the questions that carry the most weight, and a synthesis shaped for the decision at hand. The final report is explicit about where the evidence is thin.

LLM leadership training. For teams that want this capability in-house, Bursera offers training in the practice behind the work: how to direct an LLM the way you would direct a team — setting direction, reviewing the work critically, and deciding when it is ready. The training uses the same framework the research services use.

How the work is done

The full method is published on this site: an eight-part problem statement that anchors each engagement, a platform that keeps the analysis consistent across days of work and hundreds of sources, and a verification loop that runs before findings are delivered. Read it at Method. Every paper here was produced with it.
